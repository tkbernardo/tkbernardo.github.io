Tristan Bernardo

UI/UX Designer

Currently working as a UI/UX Designer for Peapod, and finishing up my master's degree in Human-Computer Interaction, at DePaul University. I love working with smart energetic people. I believe in understanding a problem space, before jumping into a solution. I enjoy collaborating with others, and I often like to sit back and observe how others break down a problem.

  • Interests: User-centered Design, Embodied Interaction, Cognitive Psychology
  • Influences: Don Norman, Design of Everyday Things
  • Background: Visual Design, Web/Print Design

Communication & Collaboration

A huge part of my job depends on effectively communicating with others, be it presenting to stake holders, or collaborating with developers. I've been fortunate to be part of great teams, and work with talented individuals.
