Ice-Burg Project

DePaul University's School of Design, in the College of Computing and Digital Media, put out a request for proposals for its inaugural Design Impact Project grant. The objective of this grant is to encourage the development of cutting-edge visual and interactive designs, that have a positive cultural or social impact. I had the honor of being on one of the teams who's project was chosen.

Shedd Aquarium Accessibility

Currently most museums do not design their exhibits to accommodate visitors with disabilities, particularly those with visual impairments. I was part of a team that investigated this problem space, and provided recommendations for improvement to the Shedd Aquarium in Chicago.
